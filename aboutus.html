Who We Are

LeoVanoDavinci’s Gaming Store Ltd is a gamer-focused company passionate about bringing the most elite gaming consoles, accessories, and experiences to customers throughout the Caribbean. We don’t just sell products — we sell the future of gaming.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate gamers, LeoVanoDavinci’s started as a small dream between friends in Trinidad. They noticed a major gap in the availability of high-end gaming products locally and set out to build a store that combined quality, affordability, and world-class service. Today, we are proud to serve both casual and pro gamers with products that power up every player's experience.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the gaming retail industry in the Caribbean by providing access to world-class technology, expert support, and unforgettable customer experiences — one gamer at a time.
